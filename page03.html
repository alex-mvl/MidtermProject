‘Smart’ by Lesserafim is the fourth track on their third mini album EASY. This Afro-beat/Amapiano inspired song shows a different sound and makes Lesserafim another group who is taking notice of the rise of popularity of Afro-beats around the world. Many people have mixed emotions since none of the producers were African, many believe that their company is just cashing out on a trend.

If you liked this song then another you might want to give a listen to is ‘Tinnitus’by TXT which is another Afro-beat inspired song!

‘Sugar Rush’ by solo artist BIBI is the second track of her Valentine’s day single‘Bam Yang Gang’. Contrasting the calm and sweet ballad of‘Bam Yang Gang’, ‘Sugar Rush’ is a quirky hip hop beat song and her unique tone makes the song even more addictive.

Another song you might like is ‘BIBI Vengeance’. This is a powerful empowering song with latin roots and a good introduction to BIBI if this is also your first time listening to her songs.
